WoundMap Team is a comprehensive wound care tool, designed to guide a team of wound care professionals in providing the best standard of care.

In order to leverage WoundMap Team's many features, we strongly encourage each member of the team to review the instructions and short videos found on our web site, and periodically reviewing the instructions available from the home view of this application.

While most of WoundMap Team's features are intuitive, there are some features, such as wound measurement, that require a review of the instructions and videos to gain the full benefits of the application.

Please take some time to visit our web site at www.theWoundMap.com
