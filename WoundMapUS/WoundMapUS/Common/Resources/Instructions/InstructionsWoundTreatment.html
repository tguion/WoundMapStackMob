Tap on the + icon located top right of the screen to create a new Wound Treatment record. You will enter a treatment section allowing documentation of wound treatment performed. A record of all treatments performed will be stored. As with most data entry sections, you can modify the status of the data record by tapping on the 'Current Status' button at the bottom of the data entry screens. Tapping the list icon bottom right will show the audit trail view.

For more detailed instructions see the WoundMap website at www.theWoundMap.com
