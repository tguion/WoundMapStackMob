WoundMap Team

A WoundMap Team allows any number of participants (users or team members) to collaborate in patient wound health care. All patient data collected by a team member will be available to other team members in real time no matter where in the world the patient or participant is located.

Once you have created a team, patient data will be available on all your devices that has WoundMap installed.

You will be the team leader, and your account will be charged a monthly subscription fee of $99. As an introductory offer, you get the first three months for the price of one.

Each team member that you add to the team will result in an additional monthly subscription fee of $99, and each team member will get the first three months for the price of one.

You must confirm payment for each team member when the monthly fee is due. If you do not confirm the charge, your team member will not be able to collarborate in team patient care.

You add a new team member (invitee) by creating an invitation using the WoundMap Team invitee's user name. Once the invitee accepts the invitation, you confirm the invitation to complete the transaction, and the monthly subscription charges will go into effect.
