WoundMap Team Member Monthly Subscription

A WoundMap Team allows any number of participants (users or team members) to collaborate in patient wound health care. All patient data collected by a team member will be available to other team members in real time no matter where in the world the patient or participant is located, as long as each device has internet access.

In addition, once you have created a team, patient data will be available on all your devices that has WoundMap installed and has a connection to the internet. The limit on the number of patients that you can manage will also be removed.

If you are creating the team, you will be the team leader, and your account will be charged a monthly subscription fee of |$99| for each team member, including yourself. As an introductory offer, each new team member will get three months of team membership for the price of the first month.

You must confirm payment for each team member when the monthly fee is due. If you do not confirm the charge, your team member will not be able to collarborate in team patient care.

You add a new team member (invitee) by creating an invitation using the WoundMap Team invitee's user name. Once the invitee accepts the invitation, you confirm the invitation to complete the transaction, and the monthly subscription charges will go into effect.
